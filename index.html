RESEARCH PROJECTS

    Fine-Tuning Google's Gemma
    [Project Link]     [Blog Series]

    Small Glimpse

    Code-Gemma is a powerful Large Language Model (LLM) fine-tuned on the state-of-the-art Gemma by Google. This project focuses on leveraging advanced fine-tuning techniques, including Supervised Fine-tuning and Parameter-Efficient Fine-tuning using LoRA (Logical Reasoning Augmentation) and QLoRA (Quantum Logical Reasoning Augmentation). The primary objective is to enhance Gemma's understanding of grounded logic in programming languages and their associated problem-solving scenarios.




    BLOGS

  • Container and Kubernetes Security

    2022 OCTOBER

    This blog discusses the evolution of application deployment from traditional Bare-Metal servers to modern container orchestration using Kubernetes. It highlights Kubernetes' self-healing capabilities, efficient scaling, and management features, along with major service providers in the field. The transition from Bare-Metal servers to containers is explored, emphasizing the benefits of containerization, such as improved resource allocation and reduced management costs. Additionally, it outlines key concepts like control-groups and namespaces in the Linux OS Kernel and popular container runtimes like Containerd and Docker Engine.


  • Descent into Kubernetes
